Approach

How I work with sound.

🎹

Composition

Melody, harmony, and rhythm as a starting point — then taken apart and reassembled into something new.

🎛

Production

Ableton Live, Bitwig, hardware synths, and a lot of deliberate restraint over what goes in and what stays out.

🎧

Mixing

Space, depth, and dynamics. A mix should feel as good as it sounds — and sound as good as it feels.
